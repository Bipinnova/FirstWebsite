Career with mySetu

At mySetu, we are working together to help our customers keep their people safe, their products sustainable and their operations productive. For more than two decades, mySetu's solutions have helped to make the world a cleaner and safer place. As mySetu continues to grow and expand, we are looking for leaders who can help shape the future of the company. You have an opportunity to join an energetic, growing software company where your input makes a difference. You'll enjoy stimulating work in a friendly environment that fosters creativity and collaboration.

Culture of mySetu

At our company, our values drive everything we do: We believe in accountability, where we take ownership of our actions. Customer delight is our goal, always aiming to surpass expectations. Innovation is key; we encourage creativity and seek novel solutions. We are dedicated to continuous learning, never ceasing to improve. Sustainability is a priority; we actively promote environmental responsibility. Finally, teamwork and trust are fundamental; we foster collaboration and uphold integrity in all our endeavors.

Benefits and perks at mySetu

At mySetu, we prioritize our employees' well-being because we understand that happy employees lead to satisfied customers. We offer competitive pay, comprehensive benefits, and special perks to support their personal, professional, and financial needs. Our benefits, like company retreats and events, an Employee Assistance Program, and wellness initiatives, contribute to a fulfilling work experience. Additionally, we provide life insurance and disability coverage, performance-based compensation, and time off for community service, ensuring our employees feel valued and supported in every aspect of their lives.
